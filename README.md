# Github Page Overview

Hi, my name is Max Naylor.

I work at Fidelity Investments Canada as an Associate in ETF Product Development. I've held this role since I finished university in 2024.
I studied at the University of British Columbia, where I completed a Bachelor of Commerce with a Finance specialization.
I passed Level 3 of the CFA program in August 2025. 

Fundamental investing is one of my personal hobbies. Most of my personal savings are in individual stocks that I pick myself.

I created this page to to have something tangible for others to learn about my interest in investing, and to document my work for personal benefit.

It will contain a selection of research reports and accompanying financial models on stocks that I have analyzed. In addition, it contains commentary on my investing approach.

If you're interested in research reports, visit the /stock-research branch of the page.

If you're interested in my investing philosophy, visit the /commentary branch of the page.

I hope you find this page useful. You can reach me via email at maxwnaylor@gmail.com
